**Project Identity:** BTG-01 (Heritage & Future)

## 1. My Philosophy: Living the Fordian Truth
Henry Ford once said: *"If I had asked people what they wanted, they would have said faster horses."*

Most people are stuck in the "now." They want slightly faster phones, slightly thinner plastics, slightly more of the same. I refuse to build "faster horses." I am building what people don’t yet know they need, but their souls crave. 

**I don't just use Ford's words; I live his philosophy in every product I create.** While the world is sprinting in a "gallop" towards disposable, soulless technology, I am stepping **Beyond the Gallop**. I am building the "engine" of a new design era.

## 2. My Craft: The Fusion of Nature and Logic
I am the bridge between the forest and the laboratory.
* **The Wood (The Soul):** I choose solid walnut. It is not a "case"; it is a living history. It has grain, it has scent, it has dignity. Like Ford's steel, my walnut is meant to last a century.
* **The Tech (The Mind):** Inside that timeless wood, I breathe life with modern hardware. I take the cold, clinical world of electronics and give it a warm, organic home.
* **The Result:** A Mirza Yüksel creation is not a "gadget" you throw away in two years. It is an heirloom that grows more beautiful as the years pass.

## 3. The Mirza Yüksel Signature
Every curve, every solder joint, and every piece of walnut is a testament to my refusal to compromise. **"Beyond the Gallop"** is not just a brand name; it is my personal promise to never settle for the "faster horse." 

I am not following trends. I am creating a legacy that honors the industrial giants of the past while outperforming the tech giants of the future.
